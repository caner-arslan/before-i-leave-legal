Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Before I Leave ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Before I Leave is designed to work completely offline. We do not collect any personal information. All data is stored only on your device:

  • App Data: All your lists, checklist items, locations, and exit session history are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personal information.
  • No Device Information: We do not collect device type, operating system version, app version, or any other device identifiers.
  • No Third-Party Data Collection: We do not use any analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

Permissions We Request

Our app requests the following permissions to provide core functionality:

  • Location: To automatically select the appropriate checklist when you're leaving from different locations (Home, Work, etc.)
  • Camera: To take photos as proof for items that require photo verification (Premium feature)
  • Photo Library: To select existing photos as proof for items that require photo verification (Premium feature)

Important: All permissions are optional. The app functions fully without any permissions granted.

Third-Party Services

Before I Leave does not use any third-party authentication services. The app works completely offline and does not require any account creation or sign-in process.

Data Storage

All your data (lists, items, locations, and session history) is stored exclusively on your device using secure storage mechanisms. We do not have any servers, and we never transmit your data to external servers. Your data never leaves your device.

Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app at any time
  • Delete: You can delete your lists, items, locations, or exit history at any time through the app settings
  • Export: You can delete your data at any time through the app settings

GDPR Compliance

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, object to processing, and data portability. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@beforeileave.app
