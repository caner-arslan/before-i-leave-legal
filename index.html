About the App

Before I Leave is a mobile application designed to eliminate the daily anxiety and mental burden of forgetting essential items when leaving home. The app provides a simple, elegant solution to a universal problem: ensuring you've checked everything and taken everything you need before you leave.

The app works completely offline - no internet connection required. All your data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers. We don't collect any personal information, making it a truly privacy-first application.
